Opportunities

Joining the Lab

We are looking for motivated students and researchers who are excited about machine learning, optimization, and impactful decision-making.

We are actively recruiting PhD students for Fall 2026. If you are interested, please read below and reach out with your CV and a brief description of your research interests.

Open Positions

🏫 PhD Students Recruiting

We are looking for PhD students with strong backgrounds in one or more of the following areas:

  • Machine learning and statistical learning theory
  • Mathematical optimization (linear, convex, integer, stochastic)
  • Operations research and decision science
  • Applied probability and statistics

A passion for real-world impact — especially in energy, infrastructure, or high-stakes domains — is a plus. Prior research experience and publications are valued but not required.

Email Prof. Zhu

📋 Research Interns / Visitors Case by Case

We occasionally host undergraduate and master's students for research internships or visiting research positions. Candidates should have solid foundations in mathematics and programming and be available for at least three months.

Inquire via Email

📄 Postdoctoral Researchers When Funded

Postdoc positions are filled on an as-needed basis. If you have a strong publication record in machine learning, optimization, or a closely related area and are interested in joining, please reach out with your CV and a statement of research interests.

Inquire via Email

How to Apply

Formal PhD applications are handled through the CMU graduate admissions portal. Before or after submitting, feel free to email Prof. Zhu directly with your CV, transcripts, and a short paragraph describing your research interests and why you want to join ADIL.
