About the lab

Bridging Learning & Decision-Making

The Adaptive Decision Intelligence Lab (ADIL) at Carnegie Mellon University investigates the intersection of machine learning, optimization, and sequential decision-making under uncertainty.

Our work spans theoretical foundations and real-world applications — from energy systems resilience to AI-assisted planning — with an emphasis on methods that are both statistically rigorous and practically deployable.
